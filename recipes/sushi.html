Sushi

Description

Sushi is fresh raw fish wrapped in a roll of rice and seaweed. It can also be served as a slice of fish placed on top of a small block of rice

  • Sushi Grade Salmon
  • Sushi Grade Tuna
  • Sushi Rice
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Mirin
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  1. Cook the sushi rice
  2. Add the rice wine vinegar, mirin, and brown sugar to the rice
  3. While the rice is cooling, slice the fish into thin rectangular slices
  4. Mold the rice into a rectangular block
  5. Place the fish on top of the rice
  6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds
  7. Enjoy with soy sauce
